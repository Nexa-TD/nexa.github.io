Frequent asked Questions

What is Checko?

Checko is a smart tool designed to help users reduce gas consumption by suggesting alternate routes and eco-friendly transportation options.

How does Checko help me save fuel?

By analyzing your usual routes, Checko recommends shorter, less congested paths or alternative transportation methods to minimize fuel usage.

Can I use Checko for daily commutes?

Yes! Checko is perfect for daily trips, whether driving, cycling, walking, or using public transport.

Does Checko provide real-time traffic updates?

Yes, Checko integrates live traffic data to suggest the fastest and most fuel-efficient routes in real time.

Can I plan trips with multiple stops?

Absolutely. Checko can optimize routes with multiple stops to save both time and gas./p>

What modes of transportation does Checko support?

Checko supports driving, cycling, walking, and public transportation, and suggests the most efficient option for each trip.

Is Checko available as a mobile app?

Yes, Checko is available for both iOS and Android devices, so you can plan eco-friendly trips on the go.

How does Checko calculate the most fuel-efficient route?

Checko considers distance, traffic, vehicle type, and alternative transportation methods to provide the route that minimizes gas consumption.

Can I use Checko without creating an account?

You can explore basic features without signing up, but creating an account unlocks personalized routes, trip history, and alerts.

Is my location data safe with Checko?

Yes, Checko prioritizes user privacy. Your location data is securely processed and never shared with third parties without your consent.
