About Checo

Our Mission

At Checo, our mission is simple: to empower consumers with clear, trustworthy information on the environmental impacts of everyday products.

We believe sustainable choices should be simple, accessible, and rewarding


We started Checo with one clear goal - to help people make smarter, more sustainable choices every day. Our team combines technology, data, and a love for the planet to deliver transparent information about how products affect the environment. We believe small decisions—made consistently—can create real impact- and we're here to make those decisions easier.
